The risk of getting it wrong

Why most landlords & businesses
are more exposed than they realise

An out-of-date, inadequate or missing FRA doesn't just put occupants at risk — it puts you personally at risk of enforcement action, prosecution and civil liability.

Your existing FRA may already be non-compliant

The Fire Safety Act 2021 and Fire Safety (England) Regulations 2022 introduced new requirements. Reports written before 2022 often don't meet current standards.

Insurers are rejecting claims citing inadequate FRAs

A fire claim denied due to a non-compliant risk assessment can cost you far more than the fine. Your insurer expects a current, competent assessment.

For HMOs and multi-let properties, the standard is higher

Houses in multiple occupation require more detailed assessments covering means of escape, fire detection and compartmentation between units.

Mixed-use buildings need coordinated assessments

Commercial below, residential above? Fire safety legislation requires responsible persons to cooperate — without that, neither is compliant.

What's included

A proper FRA — not a tick-box exercise

Every Adcap Fire Risk Assessment is conducted by a competent assessor, references current UK legislation and guidance, and delivers a clear written report you can act on.

01

Site inspection by a competent assessor

Physical visit to assess means of escape, fire detection, compartmentation, emergency lighting, fire-fighting equipment and management arrangements.

02

Full written report with risk rating

A PAS 79-aligned written report detailing identified hazards, persons at risk, current control measures and an overall risk rating.

03

Prioritised action plan

Clear, prioritised recommendations — immediate, medium and low — so you know exactly what to fix first and how to evidence compliance to your insurer or enforcing authority.

04

RRFSO & Fire Safety Act 2021 compliant

Written in accordance with the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 as amended, Fire Safety Act 2021, and Fire Safety (England) Regulations 2022.

05

Fire Safety Strategy (if required)

For new builds, conversions or planning submissions, we can provide a full Fire Safety Strategy document prepared in accordance with Approved Document B.
